# Movement Standards & Coach Cues

CrossFit-approved technique principles and SOPs for movements in the catalog. Use for **coaching intelligence** and scaling logic — not for random WOD delivery. Mechanics → Consistency → Intensity.

## Universal Priorities

1. **Mechanics** — Learn the movement correctly.
2. **Consistency** — Reliable under fatigue.
3. **Intensity** — Only then add load, speed, or volume.

## Strength / Weightlifting

### Deadlift
- **Setup**: Bar over mid-foot; hinge at hip; neutral spine; shoulders slightly in front of bar.
- **Cues**: "Push the floor away"; "Bar close to body"; "Hips and shoulders rise together."
- **Faults**: Rounded back; hips rising first; bar drifting forward.
- **Scale**: Reduce load; KB/DB deadlift; sumo for mobility limits.

### Front Squat
- **Setup**: Elbows high; bar in front rack; torso upright.
- **Cues**: "Elbows up"; "Chest up"; "Knees out."
- **Faults**: Elbows drop; forward lean; loss of midline.
- **Scale**: Goblet squat; box squat; reduce load.

### Push Press
- **Cues**: "Dip and drive"; "Finish with legs"; "Lock out overhead."
- **Faults**: Pressing before hip extension; excessive arch.
- **Scale**: Strict press; DB press; reduce load.

### Power Clean
- **Cues**: "Jump and shrug"; "Fast elbows"; "Receive in front rack."
- **Faults**: Early arm pull; poor hip extension; slow turnover.
- **Scale**: Hang power clean; DB clean; reduce load. Do not program high reps at heavy load.

## Gymnastics

### Pull-up
- **Cues**: "Full hang to chin over bar"; "Drive elbows back."
- **Faults**: Incomplete ROM; kipping without strict base.
- **Scale**: Ring rows; banded; jumping pull-ups.

### Push-up
- **Cues**: "Chest and thighs touch floor"; "Full lockout."
- **Faults**: Sagging hips; incomplete ROM.
- **Scale**: Hands elevated; knees; wall.

### Air Squat
- **Cues**: "Hip crease below knee"; "Weight in heels"; "Knees out."
- **Faults**: Knees caving; heels rise; depth.
- **Scale**: Box squat; reduce depth; assist at rig.

### Burpee
- **Cues**: "Chest and thighs touch floor"; "Full hip extension on jump."
- **Faults**: No chest contact; no full extension.
- **Scale**: Step-back; no push-up; box step-over.

## Monostructural

### Row
- **Cues**: "Legs–hips–arms"; "Smooth recovery."
- **Scale**: Pace; shorter intervals; bike/ski sub.

### Assault Bike
- **Cues**: "Push and pull"; "Controlled RPM."
- **Scale**: Lower RPM; shorter intervals; row/run sub.

### Run
- **Cues**: "Mid-foot strike"; "Tall posture."
- **Scale**: Bike/row; reduce distance; walk/jog.

### Double-Under
- **Cues**: "Wrists"; "Jump height"; "Relax shoulders."
- **Faults**: Donkey kick; arm swing; tension.
- **Scale**: Single-unders (2:1 or 3:1); line hops.

## Pairing Rules (from movements.md)

- Push + Pull; Upper + Lower; Strength + Mono; avoid double same pattern (e.g. two hinges) in a couplet.
- Triplets: aim for three different primary patterns.
